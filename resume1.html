C-Bot App

Designed and developed an intelligent control application for C-Bot, an autonomous robot engineered to clean solid plastic waste from ocean surfaces. The system is built with an emphasis on environmental safety and marine biodiversity preservation, ensuring that aquatic life remains unharmed during the waste collection process. Key features of the project include: Targeted Waste Detection: Utilizes image processing and environmental sensors to differentiate between plastic debris and marine organisms. Eco-friendly Navigation: Employs real-time path planning and obstacle avoidance to operate efficiently in dynamic marine environments. Data Logging & Reporting: Tracks volume and type of waste collected for environmental monitoring and research. Sustainability Focus: Aims to contribute to cleaner oceans by reducing plastic pollution without disrupting the aquatic ecosystem. This project combines robotics, sensor technology, environmental science, and AI-driven decision-making to address one of the most pressing environmental challenges of our time.
